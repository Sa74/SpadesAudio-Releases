## Reporting a Bug

1. Open Spades Audio > **Settings > Copy Diagnostics**
2. Click **[New Bug Report](https://github.com/Sa74/SpadesAudio-Live/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml)**
3. Paste the diagnostics into the form

You can also report directly from the app: **Settings > Report a Bug**.

## Requesting a Feature

Click **[New Feature Request](https://github.com/Sa74/SpadesAudio-Live/issues/new?template=feature_request.yml)** and describe your idea.
